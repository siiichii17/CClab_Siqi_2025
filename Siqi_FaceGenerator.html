2 – Face Generator

Design Concept:

For this project, I decided to make a pixel-style self-portrait of my face. I used simple rectangles to build my hair, squares for eyes, cheeks, and mouth. I added interaction so that when I move the mouse from left to right, my hair color will change randomly, and when I click the mouse, the mouth will become a smile, and the highlight of the eyes will also change from a square to stars. And also, my whole face will rotate too.The most fun part was the hair, because in real life I love dyeing my hair, so I wanted to show this feature by letting the hair color keep changing. The challenging part was the interaction part. I discovered that even with very simple, geometric forms, I can still show personality and emotion like I did with a smile by using a triangle. Small details, like the eye highlight or the mouth shape, make a big difference in how the face feels. If I expand this later, I might try adding blinking eyes or more backgrounds because at first I want to add when my face scale to small the background will show us more shapes, but right now I still want to figure out how to do it, but i know it’s a challenge for me, but still interesting for me during the whole time.
