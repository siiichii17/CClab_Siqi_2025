2 – Face Generator

  • For this project, I decided to make a pixel-style self-portrait of my face. I used simple rectangles to build my hair, squares for eyes, cheeks, and mouth. I added interaction so that when I move the mouse from left to right, my hair color will change randomly, and when I click the mouse, the mouth will become a smile, and the highlight of the eyes will also change from a square to stars. And also, my whole face will rotate too.
