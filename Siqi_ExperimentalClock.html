3 – Experimental Clock

  • My clock is a dandelion. It is about how I feel my own time. The middle random color squares represent the normal time, one full circle in 60 seconds, every normal. The outside triangles turn very, very fast, like when I do something I love. The happy moment also shows that it always past really fast. In my mind, it's like 3h=3s. The last circle turns very slowly, like when I do something I don’t enjoy or I don't like, it's totally opposite of a happy moment. When I click the screen, all the shapes will fall. This means time is gone and will not come back.
