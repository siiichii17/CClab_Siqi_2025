3 – Experimental Clock

Design Concept:

My clock is a dandelion clock. It is about how I feel my own time.The middle random color squares represent the normal time, one full circle in 60 seconds, every normal. The outside triangles turn very, very fast, like when I do something I love. The happy moment also shows that it always past really fast. In my mind, it's like 3h=3s. The last circle turns very slowly, like when I do something I don’t enjoy or I don't like, it's totally opposite of a happy moment.When I click the screen, all the shapes will fall. This means time is gone and will not come back.For my design process, I made three sketches. One was about normal rotation. One was about a very fast time. One was about a very slow time. I chose to combine them and come up with the idea of the dandelion clock, which can describe my own time correctly.From this assignment, I learn that a clock does not have to be only numbers. It can show my feelings at the time. I understand time can be emotional and personal.
