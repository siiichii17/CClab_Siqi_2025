5 – Optical Illusion

  • In our lecture, I worked with my classmates to test some illusion ideas. We created some interesting overlapping sketches. I put them in a .jpeg image here. For me, an illusion is something that makes people feel dizzy or confused, so I wanted to create that feeling. I used circles with changing colors and strokes to make a moving pattern. And I use for loops and if/else statements, I made one side wave and the other side rotate. It took time to balance the speed and colors to make it smooth. I think this illusion could be used as a digital art piece or even printed on our clothing. It shows how code and design can work together to create visual emotion, which is super cool.
