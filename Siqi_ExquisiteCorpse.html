4 – Exquisite Corpse

  • In this group project we exchanged our drawings, and each added a body part. My own design is a normal kid, and Lydia’s is her favorite character, and Luke’s is the big heart, which is very interesting. Then, I tried to combine them into one character. At first, it was a little hard because our styles were very different, but I learned how to balance and keep the main features from each partner. I also practiced writing user-defined functions with arguments and parameters in p5.js, which helped me make the character flexible and easy to adjust. And actually, the final result is so surprising, and I really like it:) Through this process, I realized that collaboration is not only about sharing drawings but also about exploring, being creative, and creating something new together.
